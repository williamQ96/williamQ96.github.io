# Echoes of Silicon

**A narrative-driven cyberpunk mystery.**

## Summary
A visual novel where you play as a data archaeologist uncovering the history of a fallen AI civilization.

## What & How
- **Engine**: Unity 2022 LTS.
- **Systems**: Custom dialogue system (Ink integration), branching narrative graph.
- **Art**: Hand-drawn assets + shader-based glitch effects.
